Features to be implemented in Student Management System

1. **User Authentication and Authorization:**
   - Allow users (students, teachers, and administrators) to register and log in.
   - Implement role-based access control (RBAC) to manage different user roles and permissions.

2. **Student Functionality:**
   - View personal information, such as profile details, courses enrolled, attendance, and grades.
   - Enroll in courses and view course details.
   - View and download course materials and resources.
   - Check attendance status for each course.
   - View and download course grades and transcripts.

3. **Teacher Functionality:**
   - View personal information, such as profile details and courses taught.
   - Manage course materials and resources for each course.
   - Mark attendance for students.
   - Assign and grade assignments and exams.
   - Submit final grades for courses.

4. **Administrator Functionality:**
   - Manage user accounts and roles.
   - Create, update, and delete courses.
   - Manage departments and assign teachers to departments.
   - Monitor and generate reports on student attendance, grades, and performance.
   - Handle system configuration and settings.

5. **Course Management:**
   - Create, update, and delete courses.
   - Set prerequisites for courses.
   - Assign teachers to courses and departments.

6. **Enrollment and Attendance:**
   - Allow students to enroll in courses and manage their course load.
   - Enable teachers to mark attendance for students in their classes.
   - Provide attendance reports for students and administrators.

7. **Grading and Assessment:**
   - Allow teachers to enter and update grades for assignments, exams, and projects.
   - Calculate final course grades based on assignment weights and grading scales.
   - Provide grade reports and transcripts for students and administrators.

8. **Department Management:**
   - Create and manage departments.
   - Assign teachers to departments.
   - Monitor department performance and student progress.

9. **Communication and Notifications:**
   - Implement a messaging system for communication between students, teachers, and administrators.
   - Send notifications for important events, such as course updates, assignment deadlines, and exam schedules.

10. **Search and Reporting:**
    - Enable students and administrators to search for courses, teachers, and students.
    - Generate and view reports on various aspects of the system, such as attendance, grades, and enrollment.

11. **User Profile Management:**
    - Allow users to update their profile information, including contact details and profile pictures.

12. **Dashboard and Analytics:**
    - Provide an overview dashboard for users to see their courses, grades, attendance, and upcoming events.
    - Implement analytics to track student performance and identify trends.
